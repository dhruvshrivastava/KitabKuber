Zero Cost Rental Plan from KitabKuber

What's the Zero Cost Rental Plan?
Pay the book's price at the time of purchase and once you return the book, KitabKuber will give you your money back.

What are the shipping and collection charges?
A total of 500/- (Shipping + Collection) will be deducted from the refundable amount and KitabKuber will take care of
the shipping and collection process, so that you can solely focus on your exam from the comfort of your home.


What's the rental period for this plan?
You can keep the book for as long as you want but to be eligible for the refundable amount, you'll have to
return the book within 1 year of issue. However, we don't ask for any commitments. You can keep the book
for as long as you want. No questions asked
