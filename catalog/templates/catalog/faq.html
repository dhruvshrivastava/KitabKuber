Frequently Asked Questions

What's the Zero Cost Rental Plan?
Pay the book's price at the time of purchase and once you return the book, KitabKuber will give you your money back.
Or you could keep the book forever. No questions asked.


What are the shipping and collection charges?
Shipping and Collection charges for books under 1000/- are Rs.150 each, while for books over 1000/- the charges are Rs.250 each.


What's the rental period for this plan?
You can keep the book for as long as you want but to be eligible for the refundable amount, you'll have to
return the book within 1 year of issue. However, we don't ask for any commitments. You can keep the book
for as long as you want. No questions asked


In what area does KitabKuber currently operate?
We currently operate in Delhi/NCR but we plan on expanding soon.


Is there a security deposit?
No, there is no security deposit.


What is the delivery period of the books?
The books will be delivered within 2-3 days based on the availability of the book


How do I return the book?
Just drop us an E-mail or a message on WhatsApp and we'll arrange the collection from your doorstep as soon as possible.


How's the quality of books?
We believe in keeping an updated inventory, hence all the books are new or as good as new and in proper condition


What's the exchange policy?
You can exchange the book by paying the delivery and collection charges


What if I am not satisified by the condition of the book?
We aim to offer quality service to our customers so if you're not happy with the condition of the book, you can return the book.
No questions asked


How do I sell my books on KitabKuber?
Go to the 'Sell Books' tab, fill in the details and we will contact you within 24 hours
